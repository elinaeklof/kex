The problem

Digitalisation in banking is still taking time.

Many agreements remain on paper, and the process of filling out forms on an associate's monitor — shifting it back and forth between advisor and customer — creates unnecessary friction.

01

Paperwork still dominates bank offices

Many customer agreements and onboarding forms are still completed manually, creating delays and a poor experience at branch level.

02

Associates share a single screen

The awkward back-and-forth of rotating a monitor so the customer can sign or fill in details is a daily frustration and a source of errors.

03

Digitalisation takes time — we bridge the gap

ID24 integrates with existing banking business systems without requiring changes to core infrastructure, adding a modern digital interaction layer.
