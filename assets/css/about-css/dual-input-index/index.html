Use cases

Built for real customer touchpoints

Retail & hotel

Keep sign-ups at the counter

Your sign-up registration rate is only 20% when sending SMS confirmations to customers in-store? Many in-store sign-up flows lose customers when registration continues by SMS after the interaction has already moved on.

With next-generation customer displays, the customer can complete the flow directly at the counter — improving conversion, data quality, and the overall service experience.

Banks

Give both sides their own screen

Many bank offices still rely on shared tablets for digital agreements, customer data, and signatures. Passing one device back and forth can feel clumsy, slow, and easy to get wrong.

With separate screens for the advisor and the customer, both sides can work together in a smoother, clearer, and more professional flow.
